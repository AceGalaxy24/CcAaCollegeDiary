Suprised/Sudden School Quake Drill (03/12/26)

×
×
×

On the day of the we thought was a normal class, an unexpected "Earthquake Simulation" was conducted shortly after the commencement of class. "Students" were instructed to either hold hands or use objects to shield their heads while evacuating the school in an orderly manner. Following this instruction, we exited the building and remained outside for approximately five minutes until the drill concluded. Subsequently, "Faculty Members" were requested to assemble and proceed toward the central area of the school. Later in the same day, an announcement was made to commend the students for their adherence to the safety protocols during the earthquake drill and for successfully navigating the surprise exercise.

Film Project Day#1 (03/26/26)

On the first day, "One of my Professors" announced that instead of a written exam, we would create a film project titled "Ang Trahedya Ni Bert." I volunteered to play a side character named "Eloy". The film needed to be completed by May 8. Some students agreed to act in the film, while others were assigned to make props.

During the first day of filming, I felt a bit confused—not only about the filming location but also because the script was in "Tagalog". Although I am "Filipino", I mostly speak "English" and "struggle with Tagalog". Many scenes took longer to finish due to language mistakes and forgotten lines. However, I believe we will meet the deadline and complete filming. I hope my acting skills were at least satisfactory during the shoot.
